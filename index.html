About Me

I am a dedicated freelance photographer and videographer, working since May 2023 to capture life’s most precious moments. Over the past two years, I have successfully covered 15 weddings, 5 birthdays, 3 outdoor shoots, and 3 pre-wedding shoots. My experience goes beyond solo projects — I’ve collaborated with various talented freelancing members to deliver high-quality creative work on diverse assignments.

what am i doing?

    I focus on telling authentic stories through my lens, combining technical skill with a deep passion for photography and videography. My goal is to turn every moment into a lasting memory with professionalism and creativity.
